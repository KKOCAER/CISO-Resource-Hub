# Risk Register Template

## Simple Register

| Risk | Business Process | Critical Asset | Likelihood | Impact | Current Control | Owner | Treatment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Privileged account compromise | Payments | Admin identity platform | Medium | High | MFA + PAM | IAM Lead | Improve token assurance |

## Guidance
A good risk register is:
- short
- current
- tied to owners
- tied to actual assets
- tied to treatment decisions

Avoid abstract entries with no owner or no action.
