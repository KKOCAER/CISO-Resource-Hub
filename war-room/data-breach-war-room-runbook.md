# Data Breach War-Room Runbook

## Trigger
Confirmed or strongly suspected unauthorized exposure or exfiltration of sensitive data.

## Immediate Actions
- confirm affected data sets
- classify sensitivity
- identify access path
- stop active exfiltration
- preserve investigation evidence

## Leadership Questions
- what data is affected
- who is affected
- what legal clock is running
- what customer or regulator communication may be required

## Exit Criteria
- active exposure path closed
- legal assessment stabilized
- containment evidence documented
