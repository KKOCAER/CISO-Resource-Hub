# CISO War-Room Playbook

## Purpose
Provide the executive operating rhythm for major cyber incidents.

## Activate when
- ransomware impacts critical systems
- destructive malware is suspected
- material data breach is confirmed or strongly suspected
- core business services are severely disrupted
- telco core or payment operations face credible compromise

## Roles
- CISO: incident commander
- SOC lead: detection and triage
- IR lead: containment and eradication
- IT operations: infrastructure actions
- legal and compliance: notification and liability
- communications: internal and external messaging
- business owner: operational consequence and priorities

## Golden Hour
### 0 to 15 minutes
- confirm incident status
- assign severity
- establish secure comms
- appoint scribe and timeline owner

### 15 to 30 minutes
- isolate affected scope where needed
- preserve logs and evidence
- estimate blast radius
- define immediate business protection actions

### 30 to 60 minutes
- choose containment path
- notify executive leadership
- define initial internal statement
- establish update cadence

## Non-Negotiables
- one source of truth
- no speculation in executive updates
- no recovery before containment confidence is acceptable
- legal involvement early for breach scenarios
