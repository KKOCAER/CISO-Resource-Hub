# Board Communication Guide

Boards usually do not need more cyber terminology. They need signal.

## What boards usually care about
- what changed
- what matters
- what is exposed
- what is being done
- what decision is needed

## Preferred framing
Replace:
- number of alerts reviewed

With:
- change in exposure to material business disruption

## Core reporting structure
1. current risk picture
2. major changes since last report
3. major incidents and lessons
4. investment decisions required
5. confidence gaps

## Avoid
- heat maps with no action
- technical metrics with no risk meaning
- long threat intelligence digests
