# Security Investment Prioritization

Security leaders rarely face a shortage of needs. They face a shortage of sequencing discipline.

## Inputs
- likely threat paths
- regulatory pressure
- business criticality
- current control weakness
- implementation burden

## Prioritization Lens
Score candidate investments across:
- risk reduction
- implementation complexity
- speed to value
- operational sustainability
- executive support

## Example
| Initiative | Risk Reduction | Complexity | Speed to Value | Suggested Priority |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| MFA hardening | High | Low | High | Immediate |
| Backup assurance | High | Medium | Medium | Immediate |
| Full CNAPP rollout | Medium | High | Medium | Strategic |
| UEBA expansion | Medium | Medium | Medium | Targeted |
