# Banking Use Case: Account Takeover

## Scenario
Adversary gains access to customer or privileged operational account through credential theft, session theft, or SIM-related abuse.

## Risks
- fraudulent transactions
- customer harm
- reputational damage
- regulatory scrutiny

## Control Themes
- strong identity assurance
- fraud analytics
- step-up controls
- transaction anomaly detection
- customer and SOC coordination

## Detection Ideas
- impossible travel plus new beneficiary activity
- MFA reset followed by unusual transaction
- device change plus high-risk payment behavior
