# SIEM vs XDR Decision Matrix

## SIEM is stronger when
- you need broader telemetry aggregation
- compliance and retention matter
- you need custom correlation across many domains

## XDR is stronger when
- you need faster endpoint-to-detection workflows
- you have limited detection engineering capacity
- you want built-in investigation acceleration

## Best practical answer
Many mature enterprises need both. The real question is where your detection operating model should center.

| Dimension | SIEM | XDR |
|---|---|---|
| Breadth | High | Medium |
| Endpoint depth | Medium | High |
| Compliance retention | High | Medium |
| Speed to operational value | Medium | High |
| Custom analytics | High | Medium |
